About Me

Hello, nice to meet you!

I'm Alex, a web developer based in Toronto, Ontario. I'm a hard-working individual who loves to design responsive websites with user experience using efficient code that is easy to read and mantainable. I am continously striving to improve myself and have no difficulties learning new languages and tools.

During my career in the Canadian Armed Forces and various security jobs, I have excelled in all types of communication and teamwork skills. I am a team player, but also have the ability to work independently. I am confident that I can adapt to any team environment while improving on my skills every single day, one code at a time.
